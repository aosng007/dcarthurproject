About us

Who we are.

DC Arthur Projects is a construction project management and delivery firm serving South East Queensland. We bring structure, accountability, and hands-on experience to every project — whether we're managing your consultants and contractors, or delivering the works ourselves as head contractor.

Our team has managed residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments across South East Queensland, providing clients with the oversight, reporting, and governance they need to stay in control.

Client-side project management

We act as your representative on site — managing builders, consultants, contracts, costs, and reporting so you're always in control without the day-to-day stress.

Head contracting

Where the scope suits, we take on the role of head contractor — responsible for delivering the construction works directly, with full accountability for time, cost, and quality.
